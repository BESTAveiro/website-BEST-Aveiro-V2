BEST

BEST (Best of European Students of Technology) is a non-profit and apolitical European organization of science, engineering and technology students, present in 97 universities in 34 countries with approximately 3000 members and covering approximately 1 million students.


Mission

The purpose of BEST is to develop students' abilities, by offering them the opportunity to add soft skills to their academical journey, in a multicultural environment in order to better comprehend and adapt to today's society and the global market. Best, through its events, tries to aproach students, universities and companies.

Vision

BEST's vision is based on creating an environment of diversity among students, conducive to learning, understanding and respect for different cultures and social backgrounds, as well as preparing students for a global job market. BEST believes that culturally enriched environments increase the likelihood of success of a work group.

EBEC Aveiro

BEST is the organization responsible for the organization of the EBEC - European BEST Engineering Competition - the largest engineering competition at European level. This competition is aimed at students in BEST's focus areas, and allows them to apply or enhance a wide range of social, communicative, technical skills and their ability to work as a team. There are several categories, and the LBG aveirense organizes the Team Design and Case Study.

This competition has been growing a lot and appeals to all parties, since it also serves as a platform for interaction between students and companies. It is held once during each school year.

Visit the official website

Case Study



It consists of solving a theoretical problem about technical and / or management problems, based on real facts and provided by a company. To resolve the test, you must use all the information that is provided to you. You will be able to search in external sources, if this is allowed by the regulation of your test. At the end of the challenge, your team will present the solutions found during the 24 hours of the test in a small presentation.

In addition to developing the work / discussion of ideas in team and your intellectual abilities, this will also be a great opportunity to get in touch with the partner companies of the competition.

As an example, some of the problems in this category, in previous years, are: traffic management problems in underdeveloped cities, supply chains of companies, planning of Olympic Games in cities with adverse conditions, promotion of products, etc.





Team Design



It consists of the construction of a prototype, with limited materials, to solve a proposed technical problem. The materials and tools will be provided by BEST Aveiro, as well as all the necessary information for the race. You will have to find a technical solution, together with the members of your team, within 24 hours of competition that is able to overcome the tests.

This is therefore a test that requires all your creativity, all your technical and intellectual ability to team up with a successful solution.

Examples of previous competitions: construction of a boat moved with a lamp; construction of a projectile launcher capable of overcoming various challenges.








This contest has 3 phases:

  • Local EBEC competition, organized by each LBG member;

  • National phase of EBEC. In the case of our country EBEC Portugal takes place that is a National final organized by 5 Local Groups of BEST from north to south of the country.

  • EBEC Final - European final where the winners of each country are determined.

Seasonal Course

In order to develop the students of the universities with Grupo Local BEST, several events are organized to develop their skills in an international context, establishing various networks and improving students' communication skills.

Among these events are the seasonal events, which are organized in the various seasons of the year, lasting approximately 10 days.

Dentro dos eventos sazonais incluem-se os BEST Courses, BEST Events on Education e BEST Leisure Events.

The Aveiro LBG organizes, as a rule, BEST Seasonal Courses or seasonal courses, which in addition to the aforementioned objectives, incorporate a learning component, with lectures, workshops and various activities about the theme of the course.

Presidents Meeting Bogazici in Istambul

From 8 to 14 November, the International Presidents Meeting was held in Istanbul, Turkey. This was the 23rd edition of this event which was attended by representatives of the 95 BEST local groups, the directors of BEST International, delegates from BEST international departments and members of partner associations such as EESTEC, AEGEE, BONGING and CFES .

During this event there were several presentations of projects, discussions and votes that allow the continuity and development of BEST internally. Organization

BEST Aveiro is one of the 97 local groups of BEST, a European organization of students of Sciences, Engineering and Technology, present in 97 universities of 34 countries, who work cooperatively to provide the various services that the organization offers.




The BEST local group in Aveiro was founded in 2008 under the leadership of Eric Bosne, whose objective was to develop their academic skills, share experiences and meet new people, both in Portugal and abroad, infecting some colleagues with the same will .



This is an organization of and for students, working for their personal, academic and cultural development, offering several opportunities for complementary training and exchange. To this end, BEST Aveiro organizes annually:

City

Aveiro is a picturesque Portuguese city, known as the "Venice of Portugal". Capital of the district of Aveiro, it is located in the Center region, with about 60 000 inhabitants. It was elevated to the city in 1759 by D.José I.



The attribution of the nickname "Portuguese Venice" is due to the fact that the city presents a discontinuous territory, with a large number of branches of the Ria de Aveiro to divide the territory. This zone belongs to the basin of the Vouga River, that when arriving at Aveiro is divided in numerous channels creating a unique landscape. The existence of these channels gave rise to the activity of "harvesting of moliço", which was used in the past centuries for the production of fertilizer that supplied the agricultural lands of the region. This activity was carried out using the moliceiros boats that still today can be found in the center of the city, although they are used only for tourist purposes. These boats are one of the ex-libris of this region, being highly appreciated for their colors and decoration that is usually humorous, alluding to everyday situations.


Aveiro sweets are also well known, mainly due to the soft eggs produced exclusively in the city.


Another of Aveirense's city brands are salinas, which were used for salt production even before the founding of Portugal. Due to the production and commercialization of salt, the port area of Aveiro has grown to become the main national fishing and drought site for cod. Currently the salinas are mostly deactivated, or converted to fish farms. The old salt warehouses on the São Roque canal are now being converted mainly into bars and restaurants, in order to preserve the city's cultural heritage.


Besides being recognized as a maritime city, Aveiro has a strong industrial identity and a very important port.

University

The University of Aveiro, with around 15 000 students and founded in 1973, is a nationally renowned university, as well as a European university, due to the high quality of its research in several areas (on average, each teacher publishes 1 , 5 scientific articles per year), of its faculty and of its infrastructures.
